About This Project

About the Project

DRR-AI is an open-source research tool being developed to automate the disclosure risk review process for education researchers working with IES/NCES and ICPSR restricted-use datasets.

The project is affiliated with Teachers College, Columbia University. It draws on real DRR submission experience to build an AI-powered compliance assistant that helps researchers self-check their manuscripts before submitting to official DRR channels — dramatically reducing errors, delays, and re-submissions.

DRR-AI is designed with potential for adoption within IES, university institutional review boards (IRBs), and other data security institutions that oversee restricted-use data compliance.
